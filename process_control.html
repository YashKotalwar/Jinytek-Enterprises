Process Control

The operators of blasting systems can rapidly check their own blasting process with straightforward useful tests:

  • By methods for repellent tests with water, the purported water repellent test, the grease substance of the blasting medium can be evaluated.
  • With the adhesive tape test, the leftover residue on impacted surfaces can be resolved.
  • With the water test on the blasted surface, the blasting results can be checked, in light of the wettability of the surface of the work piece. All around cleaned work pieces made of mild steel show rust inside a brief timeframe.
  • Also, hand rub test, smell of the blasting medium and visual shading assessment of the blasting medium and blasting metal surface can give an early introduction.
