Alluminium Cut Wire

Aluminum Cut wire shots are essentially produced using aluminum alloy wire with the assistance of exceptional cutting machines. Aluminum cut wire shot is accessible in Grade EN AW-5019 (EN AW-AlMg5). They are exceptionally sturdy and leaves moderate buildup.


APPLICATIONS

Cleaning aluminum or other non-ferrous castings that require a satin type finish while as yet eliminating separating lines or other blemishes. It is utilized for Aluminum Die Castings Shot blasting, Magnesium Die Castings Shot blasting, Aluminum Forgings and so forth.


SPECIFICATION

Sizes:      .032″ – .080″ (0.80mm – 2.0mm)
Wire:      Grade – EN AW-5019 (EN AW-AlMg5)
Hardness Scale:      110 – 140 HV
Density:      8.6 g/cm³
