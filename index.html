About Us

Established in the year 2014. We “JINYTEK ENTERPRISES” are one of the renowned manufacturers, Importers and suppliers of a diverse range of metal abrasives including cast steel shots, cast steel grits, steel cut wire shots, stainless steel shots, stainless steel cut wire shots and other industrial abrasives used for Surface Preparation prior to Painting ,Coating , rust removal, scales and Shot Peening of heat treated components. The offered range finds applications in field such as Ferrous and Non ferrous Foundries and forgings , Automobiles Industry, Railways, fasteners Industry, Aluminum Extrusion etc. Apart from this We are representing PantaTec GmbH, Germany all over India, who is manufacturer of PantaTec ULTIMATE additive for cleaning all metallic blasting media and for the improvement of blast results in blasting system. This Cleaning Additive cleans Shots, Oil and Grease while shot blasting / shot peening and eliminate Fire Problems arising from Shot Blasting Dust.
